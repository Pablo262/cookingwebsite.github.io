How to prepare fish fillets

Ingredients

  • A bottle of oil
  • Thin salt
  • 1 or 2 fish fillets
  • A pan

Steps

  1. Prepare a pan and put it on a high temperature for 3 minutes
  2. Add a small amount of oil
  3. As soon as the oil begins to smoke, salt the fillets and place them in the pan
  4. Make sure to lower the heat to medium if the filets are thick, or keep it high if the fillets are thin
  5. Let the fish cook for 3 minutes, and then flip it and let it cook for 1 minute
